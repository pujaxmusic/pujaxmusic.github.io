About Pujax

The Journey of Electronic Passion

Hey, I'm Pooya

An electronic music enthusiast currently based in Berlin. My thing is basically anything electronic. I use Ableton Live for my music production.

I've been into music since I was 9, learning music theory and later picking up the guitar. In my teenage years, I played around with friends, covering and producing music. It was a learning experience that fueled my passion.

Now in Berlin, I'm all about producing electro music. Follow me on this journey of beats and vibes! 🎧🔥
